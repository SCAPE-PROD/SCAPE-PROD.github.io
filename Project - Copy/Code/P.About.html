  • WHO I AM

    My name is Sam (S-CAPE), I'm a small music producer from the PNW. I began producing as an outlet for channeling my creative energy and quickly became encapsulated by the process. Music has always played a significant role in my life. I am currently in college at Gonzaga University, so music is very much a side-project during the semester (hoping for it to become the main-project). Producing music brings me an indescribable sense of bliss and accomplishment much greater than only listening to music. I produce hardstyle, but I also don't like to limit my future-self just to one box.

    Never be afraid to like “mysterious music”, especially in an unwelcome environment.

  • WHAT I USE

    FL STUDIO

    I highly recommend the FL Studio Producer Edition for anyone looking to begin music production. It's not too hand-holdy but at the same time, not too intimidating. You can typically score a pretty good discount ($199 $149) around Black Friday for all editions.


    VITAL WAVETABLE

    Vital is a free 3 oscillator synthesizer wavetable with many features including filters, LFOs, distortion, reverb, and more. Basically, all the stuff you need but in the form of a free alternative. It's VERY intimidating at first but can essentailly create any noise possible.


    PIONEER FLX-4 CONTROLLER

    If you want to get into mixing with or without already producing, the FLX-4 is the way to go. It's pretty simple to get into and use but still has many effects, controls, and features for a 2-channel controller. A new one will cost around $300, but there is a thriving market of used DJ equipment.
